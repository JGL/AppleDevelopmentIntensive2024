Joscha Bach and a Computational Universe.
Joscha Bach is a researcher in Cognitive Science & Artificial Intelligence.
He's done a series of talks at the annual Chaos Communication Congress.
My favourite talk of his is titled "Machine Dreams - Dreaming Machines" is concerned with the idea that the basis of the entire universe is computational. His talk discusses a computational approach to Epistemology (what the fuck?), Metaphysics (why the fuck?) and finally Ontology (the fuck?). Let's watch a little of his talk and have a discussion about it.
